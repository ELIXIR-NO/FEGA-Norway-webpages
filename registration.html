Registration

This page outlines the steps to create an account in Central EGA. If you encounter any difficulties during the registration process or require additional assistance, you can reach out to us at fega-norway-support@elixir.no.

1. Central EGA registration

Firstly, you'll need to register as a new user in Central EGA .

2. Notify our helpdesk.

Your registration is now pending approval. Please notify the FEGA Norway helpdesk of your application.

3. Activate account

Congratulations! Your EGA account is ready to be activated. Last thing you need to do is to check your email for an activation link

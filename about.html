Norwegian Federated EGA node

About

Team

Sensitive Data Archive (aka LocalEGA) project has emerged as part of a productive collaboration between ELIXIR Norway, NeIC Tryggve and our partners at Central EGA: EBI and CRG. The Norwegian Node is developed and maintained by ELIXIR Norway and UiO (USIT and TSD).

ELIXIR Norway is a national node of ELIXIR, the pan-European infrastructure for biological information, supporting life science research and its translation to medicine, environment, the bioindustries and society.

Codebase

All the software components incorporated into SDA solution are open-source and publicly available at GitHub. We leverage the power and flexibility of various technological stacks, therefore the list of languages used includes Python, Java, and Go.
